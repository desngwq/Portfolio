About Desmond

Biography

  • Introduction: I am a Diploma in Accountancy with Certificate in Software Programming and Applications (CSPA) graduate from Singapore Polytechnic. I love figures and that would best explains why I took up accountancy in polytechnic. To pursue my aspirations to be accounting software consultant, I decided to further my studies in the field of Computer Science to build on my knowledge that I have gained from CSPA. In 2011, I have made the decision to make a shift from accounting to information technology field by pursuing a Bachelor of Electronic Commerce in National University of Singapore.
  • Date of Birth: 28th September 1988
  • Nationality: Singaporean
  • Degree Qualification: Bachelor of Computing (Electronic Commerce), Honours
  • Diploma Qualification: Diploma in Accountancy

Portfolio

SAP Asia Pte Ltd, Intern
(Period: May 14 – Aug 14)

Proposed and performed User Interface overhaul with modern UI design for JAM (SAP’s Enterprise Social System created for knowledge sharing amongst SAP employees) with the aim of improving end-user experience.


Launched JAM together with Deal Support Lead. Created user manual and conducted demonstration for over 20 SAP employees, to ensure the seamless adoption of the system.


Initiated a group discussion with JAM’s end-user representatives from other regional offices to collect feedback and implemented suggestions to improve the overall user experience of JAM.


Created a Licence Revenue Recognition Checklist that was previously created in Microsoft Infopath using Microsoft Excel together with a colleague in Tokyo. The Excel version is enhanced with new features (such as user authentication and timestamp), which were the shortcomings in the Infopath version previously.

National Service, Admin Support Assistant
(Period: Jun 09 – Jun 11)

Managed and coordinated reservist exercise for reservist personnel.


Managed and supervised a reservist unit of 2000 reservist personnel and handled reservist personnel with issues their Individual Physical Proficiency Test (IPPT).


Developed an event registration program that captures the attendance of the personnel.


SAP Asia Pte Ltd, Intern
(Period: Feb 08 – Apr 08)

Prepared and reported costing for various cost centers on SAP system from Microsoft Excel.


Reviewed travel expenses claim with travel cancellation refund and identified and reported mismatch to the Finance Department for next course of action.


Computed and tallied payments made for each client’s account with its account balance.
